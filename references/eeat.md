# E-E-A-T Review

Use this file for trust and content quality reviews.

`E-E-A-T` is easiest to score as:

- Experience
- Expertise
- Authoritativeness
- Trustworthiness

Trust is the most important one.

## Strong Signals

### Experience

- first-hand examples;
- original screenshots or photos;
- real case studies;
- specific details that feel lived, not generic.

### Expertise

- clear author name;
- relevant background or credentials;
- accurate explanations;
- sources where needed.

### Authoritativeness

- mentions, citations, interviews, awards, or strong portfolio;
- deep topical coverage;
- recognition in the niche.

### Trustworthiness

- clear contact details;
- about page;
- privacy policy and terms;
- HTTPS;
- honest claims;
- return, refund, or service details where relevant.

## Weak Signals

- anonymous content;
- no dates;
- no examples or proof;
- generic text that could fit any site;
- repeated template copy across many pages.

## Score Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 90-100 | very strong trust and expertise |
| 70-89 | good but not complete |
| 50-69 | average, clear gaps |
| 30-49 | weak |
| 0-29 | critical trust problem |

## First Fixes

If the page is weak:

1. add a visible author or business owner;
2. add proof, examples, or screenshots;
3. add sources or facts;
4. remove generic filler text.
