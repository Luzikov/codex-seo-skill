# Local SEO

Use this file for clinics, repair services, salons, restaurants, offices, and service-area businesses.

## What To Check

- consistent business name, address, and phone;
- service pages;
- real location pages;
- reviews and proof;
- contact details and hours;
- `LocalBusiness` schema when appropriate.

## What A Good Location Page Needs

- a real city or area target;
- services tied to that location;
- local reviews, photos, or proof;
- contact details;
- real local details, not a generic template.

## Risk Signs

- dozens of city pages with near-identical copy;
- no local proof;
- no sign of real business presence.

## Usual Fixes

- add or fix `LocalBusiness` schema;
- clean up contact consistency;
- strengthen service and location pages;
- collect better reviews;
- improve contact and service-area details.
