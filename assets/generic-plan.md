# Generic SEO Plan

## Priority Pages

- homepage
- about
- services or categories
- contact
- blog or resources

## First Steps

1. fix indexing and crawl blockers;
2. clean up `title`, `meta description`, `H1`, and canonicals;
3. strengthen core money pages;
4. add basic schema: `Organization`, `WebSite`, `BreadcrumbList`;
5. improve internal linking.

## Main Metrics

- organic traffic;
- clicks and impressions;
- growth of target pages;
- leads or sales from organic traffic.
