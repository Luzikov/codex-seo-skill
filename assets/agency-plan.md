# Agency SEO Plan

## Priority Pages

- homepage
- service pages
- case studies
- industry pages
- team
- contact
- blog
- comparison pages

## Main Priorities

1. service pages with clear outcomes;
2. case studies with numbers;
3. niche or industry pages;
4. content around buyer pain points.

## Main Schema

- `Organization`
- `Service`
- `Person`
- `Article`
- `BreadcrumbList`

## Main Metrics

- leads;
- growth of service pages;
- growth of case studies;
- visibility for niche terms.
